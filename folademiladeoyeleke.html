About CANVAS FACE

This web page displays a cartoon-like face on the canvas

  • You can use thebuttons to change the expression of the face;you can switch between neutral,happy and angry
  • Click on the nose to make the eyes open wider.(clicking this twice will the make the eyes return to relatively the same way they were before)
  • Click on the chin to make a beard appear.(clicking this twice will undo the beard)
  • Yes you can make the face have wide eyes and a beard.
This program has been optimized for mobile devices by scaling down the canvas and simplifying the content, ensuring a responsive and visually appealing experience on all devices.
